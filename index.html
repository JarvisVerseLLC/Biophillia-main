Why We Are Different?

The basic economic law of supply and demand dictates that if the supply of something decreases, then the price will have to rise, assuming demand remains constant. This is the reason why Satoshi Nakamoto programmed the Bitcoin Reward Protocol to “halve” every four years, which reduces the block reward for miners by 50%. Thus, fewer bitcoins enter circulation.

We incorporate this reward algorithm to BitcoinDefi Staking Protocol and will be supported by a burning mechanism, and while other developers declared that their token is deflationary, their supplies are continuously rising tremendously, our token is 100% deflationary in nature and have a maximum supply of 50,000,000, followed by making a lot of platforms that will create a huge demand of our token. Creating Decentralize Exchange, NFT Marketplace, Metaverse and Real World use case is one of our out most priority to keep the demand continuously rising as well as the price.
